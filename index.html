Artificial Intelligence main page

In computer science, artificial intelligence (AI), sometimes called machine intelligence, is intelligence illustrated by machines, in contrast to the natural intelligence shown by people and creatures. Leading AI textbooks characterize the field as the study of "intelligent agents": any gadget that sees its environment and takes actions that maximize its chance of successfully accomplishing its goals.
